PromptSpy: Android Malware Meets Generative AI

Summary of the report via Android Authority

What is PromptSpy?

Researchers have identified a new strain of Android spyware dubbed PromptSpy. While traditional spyware focuses on stealing photos and messages, PromptSpy adds a modern twist by leveraging Googleâ€™s Gemini AI to automate and refine its data exfiltration process.

Key Findings

  • AI Integration: The malware uses Gemini to analyze stolen data, helping attackers identify high-value information without manual sorting.
  • Infiltration Method: Often disguised as legitimate-looking apps (like PDF viewers or system updates) sideloaded from third-party sources.
  • Data Targets: Focuses on SMS logs, contact lists, device locations, and specific document types.

How to Stay Safe

To protect your device from threats like PromptSpy, experts recommend:

  1. Stick to the Play Store: Avoid downloading APKs from unknown websites.
  2. Check Permissions: Be wary of simple apps asking for "All File Access" or "Accessibility Services."
  3. Keep Play Protect On: Ensure Google Play Protect is active to scan for known malicious signatures.
